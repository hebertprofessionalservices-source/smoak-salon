About Smoak

A Jackson salon built around skill, comfort, and guest care.

Smoak Salon has been part of Jackson's Fondren District since 2009, shaped by education, teamwork, and a belief that luxury should feel relaxed.

Casual luxury, never cold.

Our work is polished, but the room is easy to settle into. Guests describe Smoak as relaxing, clean, contemporary, eclectic, professional, and genuinely welcoming. The goal is not to rush you into a chair. It is to understand what you want, what your hair needs, and what maintenance will actually fit your life.

During a first visit, your stylist will talk through your hair history, goals, timing, maintenance, and starting price before the service begins. That clarity matters, especially for color, natural hair, smoothing, and extensions.

Values

What guests can expect.

Texture-aware service

Natural hair, curls, silk presses, trims, color, and styling are approached with respect for long-term hair health.

Inclusive comfort

Smoak is a diverse, comfortable salon for different textures, styles, ages, and client needs.
