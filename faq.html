FAQ

Good answers before you book.

Still unsure? Call the salon and we will help you choose the right appointment.

How do I book an appointment?

Book online through GlossGenius or call 601-982-5313.

Where is Smoak Salon located?

We are at 622 Duling Ave, Jackson, MS 39216 in the Fondren area.

Do you work with curly hair?

Yes. We work with curls and can help with shape, definition, styling, and maintenance.

Do you work with natural hair?

Yes. We serve natural hair guests, including 4C hair, silk presses, twists, wash n gos, treatments, and healthy trims.

Do you offer silk presses?

Yes. Silk presses and silk presses with treatment are available with starting prices based on stylist, length, density, and timing.

Do you offer men's haircuts?

Yes. Smoak offers men's cuts, fades, and detailed finishing.

Do you offer balayage?

Yes. Balayage and gloss services are available, often with consultation guidance for color goals and maintenance.

Are prices starting prices?

Yes. Starting prices are shown clearly, and your stylist will talk through timing, maintenance, and any add-ons before the service begins.

Do you offer consultations?

Yes. Consultations are recommended or required for extensions, color corrections, major changes, smoothing services, and some texture services.

Do you do extensions?

Yes. Extension consultations, tape-in install and maintenance, and traditional sew-in services are available by plan.

Do you offer bridal or formal styling?

Yes. Formal styling, makeup application, and bridal makeup are available.

What should I expect at my first appointment?

A thoughtful consultation, clear plan, starting price conversation, and a stylist who explains the process before beginning.
