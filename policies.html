Policies

Clear expectations make better appointments.

Our policies are designed to protect guest time, stylist time, and the quality of the work.

Appointments

Book online or call the salon. Some services may require a consultation, deposit, or card on file.

Cancellations

Please give at least 24 hours notice when possible. Services reserved without adequate notice may be subject to a cancellation fee.

Late arrivals

If you are running late, call us. We will do what we can, but late arrivals may need a shorter service or rescheduling.

Starting prices

All prices are starting points. Your final price may vary by stylist level, hair length, density, timing, product usage, and plan.

Consultations

Color corrections, major changes, smoothing, extensions, and some natural hair services may require a consultation before booking the full service.

Children

Children are welcome when scheduled for a service. For safety and comfort, please arrange childcare for longer appointments when possible.

Service adjustments

If something does not feel right, contact us within 7 days so we can talk through the best next step.

Product purchases

Ask your stylist about take-home care. Product returns or exchanges may vary based on condition and salon policy.
